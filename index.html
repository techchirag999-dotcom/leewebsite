About Lee Hydraulics
& Fasteners

Founder's Vision

A vision forged
in precision.

Founded in 1991 by Rajesh Kumar Lee, Lee Hydraulics & Fasteners (P) Ltd. began as a precision machining unit in Hyderabad with a commitment to deliver components that meet the world's most exacting standards. Three decades later, that commitment has earned global recognition.

Today Lee is a trusted partner to leading mining, construction, and energy companies worldwide — exporting precision DTH hammers, hydraulic fittings, and fasteners to 17+ countries.

Rajesh Kumar Lee
Founder & Managing Director
