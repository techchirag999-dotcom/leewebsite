About Lee Hydraulics

Founder's Vision

A vision forged
in precision.

Established in 1985, LEE HYDRAULICS & FASTENERS (P) LTD. is driven by innovative ideas, dedicated pursuance, and a commitment to quality and reliability. We are proud to be the first in India to establish indigenous production of critical machine alloy fasteners, including maraging steels, Titanium Alloys, and nickel-based superalloys to International Standards.

Under the guidance of our Managing Director, Chindam Venkatesh, we have pioneered R&D in manufacturing fasteners for aerospace and medical applications. Our motto remains clear: to deliver the best quality, fast service, and cost-effective products to our global partners.

Chindam Venkatesh
Managing Director

Hydraulic
